Product Description

The S3 is EZVIZ’s latest 4K action camera, shoots videos in 4K quality at 24 fps. By combining features like ultra-wide angle aspherical lens, intuitive IPS touch screen, WDR technology and distortion correction, the S3 help you record the world exactly as you see it.The S3 captures incredible 4K* video at 24fps, scoring perfect shots in crisp, high-definition image quality.With a 150°ultra-wide viewing angle and 6 layers of full glass lens, the S3 covers more and prevents de-focusing.Combined with specialized correction algorithm, adjustable FOV and ultra-wide angle aspherical lens with distortion correction, the S3 automatically adjusts for any image distortion.With Built-in Bluetooth and Wi-Fi, the S3 enables integration with mobile apps and controllers. You can easily preview, control and share the images and videos via EZVIZ SPORTS app.

Features:

  • 4K Video and 8MP Wide Photo With 1/2.8" CMOS sensor, S3 supports up to 4K* Video at 24fps and 8MP Wide Photo in various shooting mode. The resolution is adjustable as you wish. Also you can enjoy extra fun with built-in filters.
  • 150° Ultra Wide Angle with Less Distortion: With 6 layers of full glass lens and 150° Ultra Wide Angle, S3 covers more and prevents de-focusing. The specialized correction algorithm adjusts FOV and image distortion.
  • Advanced Low-Light Mode: By simply switching to the auto low light mode*, the S3 presents images with intense detail and clarity, better brightness even in the dark. *Low-Light Mode can be enabled only at framerates above 30fps
  • Outstanding Night Shots with WDR: Thanks to WDR technology, the S3 handles high-contrast lighting with ease for outstanding night shots.
  • 40m Waterproof Housing and Mount Kit: Take the waterproof case and mounting accessories included, you can enjoy the fun in variety of scenarios including surfing, diving, snorkeling, skiing, riding, skating, etc.
  • 2 Hours Battery Life on 4K/24fps Mode: The S3 supports MicroSD card up to 256GB(Requires Class 10 and above). With 1160mAh rechargable battery, it can record up to 2 hours on 4K/24fps mode once.
